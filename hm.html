H&M Summer Spots

Social Media ⋅ Acne Production Stockholm

H&M tasked us about coming up with a concept for spreading awareness of their upcoming summer collection through the use of social media.

We created an interactive contest where users, by using Instagram, could submit geo-tagged pictures of their favourite spots to hangout during the summer months. Together these pictures created an interactive map of Summer Spots. The Summer Spots were later voted on by users, and the winners received selected items from the collection.
