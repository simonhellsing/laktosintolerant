Adidas Stan Smith

Design ⋅ Freelance

To celebrate the relaunch of the Adidas classic Stan Smith sneaker, a party was held at a prestigious nightclub in central Stockholm.

During the evening a photographer took pictures of the attendees. These pictures were then sent off to be redesigned in the iconic style of the Stan Smith sneaker. The partygoers were then given the chance to take their picture home with them.

Together with a team over at The Viral Company I worked with designing and delivering the 'Stanified' pictures that were handed out during the evening.
