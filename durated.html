Durated

UI/UX Design ⋅ Berlin

Durated was a Berlin based startup that focused on highlighting and selling eco-friendly fashion and interior design and telling the stories behind the creators of the products.

During my time at Durated I ended up being the only Designer in the team, and subsequently headed up a complete redesign of the entire webshop and iOS App by myself. Besides UI Design my responsibilities also included prototyping and user testing both the website and the iOS App.
