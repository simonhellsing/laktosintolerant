Canon Project Imagination

Digital ⋅ B-Reel New York

Project Imagination was a contest site where users where given the chance to submit photos to be narrowed down by Canon, Ron Howard and community voting. Out of the 10 winning photos 10 short films were produced, 5 of which were directed by celebrity icons and showcased at a special Canon Project Imagination Film Festival.

The celebrity icons were two-time Academy Award® winner Ron Howard, Eva Longoria, Jamie Foxx, Biz Stone, Georgina Chapman and James Murphy.

I worked as a designer on elements of the site and assisted with mobile device optimization.
