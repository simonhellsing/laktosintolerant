Wimdu

UI/UX Design ⋅ Berlin

Wimdu is a marketplace that offers short-term holiday accommodation, serving as a middle point to connect travellers and hosts in over 2000 cities around the world.

As a UI/UX Designer I use user research methods including card sorting, prototyping, usability testing, user interviews and personas for validating design decisions, and creating finished designs that are consistent with the Wimdu brand.
